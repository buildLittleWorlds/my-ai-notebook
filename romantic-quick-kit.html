Romantic Quick Kit

How to use this

The concepts in this kit are interconnected. Many Romantic writers and thinkers shared a central story: a journey that begins in a state of primal unity, falls into a painful state of division and alienation, and concludes by returning home to a higher, more self-aware state of integration. Think of it as a great circle or spiral. The pages below explore the key features of this journey.

  • Use the Hermeneutics page as your reading method; the other pages are what to look for as you read.
  • Every page links out to sources you can look into further.
