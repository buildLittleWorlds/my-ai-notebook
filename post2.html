The book laid out a coherent thesis, and I worked on developing that thesis in a consistent way. But I sent it off to the publisher, and I started noticing fuzzy or just plain mixed-up things. A bunch of things might just be wrong. So I built a site with imaginary reviews and critiques of the book to help me think about it.
